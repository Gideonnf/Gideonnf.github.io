About me

A little bit about me, I graduated from Game Development and Technology in Nanyang Polytechnic's School of Interactive and Digital Media. Currently aiming to enter into university after I have finished my National Service. I love working together with people to create something, whether it is a game or not. If it helps people or provides entertainment, it provides me with a sense of fulfilment knowing that I was part of the team that made that.

I have always had an interest in games since young, playing them throughout my childhood. Starting with games like Warcraft 3 the Frozen throne, to even games like Club penguin. It's because of these games that has inspired me to work in the game industry.

I also enjoy playing sports occasionally and am also trying to learn how to draw and model at the side. Mainly so that I can start to prototype my own games.
